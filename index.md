We are a **non-profit association** dedicated to researching and analyzing Internet threats. We're a global community of more than **100 researchers** who've been working together for over a decade.

We analyze malware, publish reports, organize workshops, and organize our **annual conference** in Palma de Mallorca. Above all, **we share knowledge** because we believe digital security is everyone's business.

We're a vetted community focused on serious research but with a human touch. Here you'll find technical analysis and real stories from the cybersecurity trenches.

**Welcome to the trenches.**
